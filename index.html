Home

Welcome to the Troop 367 website.

We meet weekly at the Christ Episcopal Church at 14 School St. Medway, MA.

Our meetings begin at 7:00 pm and end at 8:00 pm.

We are a Boy lead Troop.  This means that the Boys in the Troop run the meetings and decide what events and trips we do, and the Adults are here to guide us.

If you are interested in joining our Troop you can contact us by clicking on the Contact Our Troop link at the top of this page
